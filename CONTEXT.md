# PAIN TAXI

PAIN TAXI is a competitive arcade driving game about earning fares under escalating pressure in a neon city.

## Language

**Endless Run**:
A standalone single-player session made of consecutive shifts that increase in difficulty and continue until the player fails a cash quota.
_Avoid_: Campaign, match

**Shift**:
A short timed round within an Endless Run in which the player earns cash by completing fares.
_Avoid_: Level, stage

**Cash Quota**:
The minimum cash the player must earn during a Shift to continue the Endless Run.
_Avoid_: Winning cash target, score target

**Fare**:
A passenger job that begins at a pickup zone and pays cash only after successful delivery, with reckless driving reducing or forfeiting the payout.
_Avoid_: Mission, quest

**Rival**:
An AI taxi driver that competes for fares and disrupts the player during a Shift without directly determining whether the Endless Run continues.
_Avoid_: Enemy, bot

**Pit Stop**:
The paused intermission after a cleared Shift where the player may spend earned cash on recovery before starting the next Shift.
_Avoid_: Shop, results screen
